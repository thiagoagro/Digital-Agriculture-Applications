## Research Profile

Ph.D. Candidate with experience in **precision and digital agriculture**, **machine learning**, **data science**, and **remote sensing**. Solid experience with UAV imagery (RGB, multispectral, hyperspectral), spray drones, variable rate technology, and satellite image analysis. Research includes ML models for yield, crop height, and physiology parameter prediction, as well as deep learning approaches for object detection (YOLO) in agricultural imagery.

International experience as a **Visiting Researcher at the University of Georgia (UGA)** in 2024, collaborating with interdisciplinary teams across agronomy, remote sensing, and data science.

## Research Philosophy

I am especially interested in building solutions that move beyond experimental plots and can be translated into **usable tools for growers, researchers, and field technicians**. That means combining model performance with interpretability, scalability, and operational practicality.
