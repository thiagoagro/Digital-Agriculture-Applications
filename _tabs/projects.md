## Research Highlights

### 1. Peanut Maturity Prediction with AI
**Goal:** Predict peanut maturity using climatic variables and vegetation indices.

**Methods:** Machine learning, feature engineering, remote sensing inputs, and agronomic interpretation.

**Why it matters:** Supports harvest timing decisions in environments where field-level maturity assessment is costly and time-sensitive.

---

### 2. Corn Plant Detection from UAV Imagery
**Goal:** Detect and count corn plants under different soil backgrounds, growth stages, and flight heights.

**Methods:** YOLO-based object detection, UAV imagery preprocessing, and evaluation across variable field conditions.

**Why it matters:** Moves computer vision closer to robust real-world deployment in crop monitoring.

---

### 3. Drone Spraying Optimization in Coffee Systems
**Goal:** Understand how flight height, volume, and nozzle type affect spraying performance in Arabica coffee.

**Methods:** Experimental design, application engineering, image-based and agronomic evaluation.

**Why it matters:** Improves technical decisions in drone-based crop protection operations.

---

### 4. Satellite and Weather Data for Yield Intelligence
**Goal:** Use multispectral data, vegetation indices, and climatic information to support crop yield estimation.

**Methods:** Satellite analytics, weather integration, regression and machine learning models.

**Why it matters:** Enables more scalable crop intelligence in regions with limited surface monitoring infrastructure.
