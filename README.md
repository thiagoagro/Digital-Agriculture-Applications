# Digital Agriculture Applications

> Bridging agronomy, data science, and remote sensing to build practical solutions for precision agriculture.

---

## About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Agronomy (Crop Science) at the [Federal University of Lavras (UFLA)](https://ufla.br/), Brazil, focused on **Precision and Digital Agriculture**. My research integrates machine learning, deep learning, and satellite/UAV remote sensing to solve real-world agricultural challenges — from predicting crop maturity and yield to automating plant detection with computer vision.

In 2024, I spent 12 months as a **Visiting Researcher** at the [University of Georgia (UGA)](https://www.uga.edu/), USA, in the Crop & Soil Sciences Department, where I collaborated with interdisciplinary teams in agronomy, remote sensing, and data science.

**Research Interests:** Machine Learning for crop prediction, YOLO-based object detection from UAV imagery, satellite-based vegetation indices, variable-rate technology, and spray drone applications.

**Tools & Skills:** Python (scikit-learn, PyTorch, Ultralytics, pandas) | R (tidymodels, caret, ggplot2) | QGIS | Pix4D | Google Earth Engine | Google Colab | Git/GitHub
